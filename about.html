About Us

Sanatan Dharma Foundation®

Sanatan Dharma Foundation® is a registered not-for-profit, non-political public Religious and Charitable Trust, having its head office at Gurugram (Haryana) and branch offices at Mumbai, Delhi and Lucknow.

Its main objective is to build a Sanatan Dharma Holy City for the resurgence of Sanatan Dharma and uniting all Hindus. The Holy City shall be the largest place of Pilgrimage, Knowledge, Worship and Prayers in the world, even bigger than the Vatican City and Holy Mosque at Mecca.

Aims and objectives

As per the Trust Deed of the Foundation, the following are the aims and objectives:

Aims

To research and disseminate knowledge about the philosophy of Sanatan Dharma.

To build a place of pilgrimage based on the philosophy of Sanatan Dharma.

Place of pilgrimage to have all kind of facilities that the Trustees deem fit.

To work for the Resurgence of Sanatan Dharma.

To contribute in building the character of the people at large.

To become a central body for issuing guidelines for way of life following Sanatan Dharma

To have interface with believers of other religions / faiths for peaceful co-existence

To inculcate pride in Hindu customs, traditions, way of worship, festivals, rituals etc.

To create teachers who shall educate coming generations on Sanatan Dharma.

To educate on Sanatan Dharma philosophy and way of living and demonstrate that the world is one big family.

To do any kind of charitable activity that the Trustees deem fit in the fields of education, uplifting of women and girl child.

Supporting younger generation for setting up businesses/professions, healthcare, agriculture, horticulture or in any other field or activity

Objectives

To achieve the above Aims, the Trust shall have following Objectives:

To organize debates, discussions, seminars, campaigns, symposiums and lectures for aims and objectives of Trust.

To create awakening among people of India and world at large about Sanatan Dharma.

To support individuals, institutions, organizations who can help in achieving the aims and objectives of the Trust
